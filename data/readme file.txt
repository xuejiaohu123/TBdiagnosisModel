The data in three files have been preprocessed and included features used for modeling.

The files 'Selection'  and 'Validation' refer to the data in the selection and the validation cohort, respectively. The file 'Allpatients' refers to the data from the overall the selection and validation cohorts.

The lncRNA data are the log2-transformed expression of ENST00000497872, n333737, n335265.

The abbreviation: RBC, red blood cells; HB, hemoglobin; WBC, white blood cells; L, lymphocytes; M, monocytes. 

The column of 'Group_value' is equivalent to the column of 'Group'. 0 refers to non-TB Disease controls; 1 refers to NPTB patients.

The file 'Allpatients' refers to the data from the overall the selection and validation cohorts.
